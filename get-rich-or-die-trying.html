--- layout: page title: Get Rich or Die Trying description: Solo project for the GMTK 2022 Game Jam ---

A puzzle game built over 48hrs, inspired by the theme “Roll of the Dice”

Key Achievements The first game I actually felt proud to put out into the world. Nicely polished, with clean graphics and undo functionality well-received by fellow game jammers. By no means perfect, but shows how far I've come from before my Master's.

Made With Godot, C#.

Controls Arrow keys to move. Z to undo, R to restart.

The original game jam entry can be found at Itch.io.
